Introduction to Computer Science

Course Description

The Introduction to Computer Science course provides a comprehensive foundation in the principles of computer science. Students will learn about algorithms, data structures, programming languages, software development, and the theoretical foundations of computing. This course is designed for students who are new to computer science and serves as the starting point for further study in the field.
